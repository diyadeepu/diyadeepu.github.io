About Me

I'm a freshman at Oregon State University Honors College, pursuing a degree in Computer Science with a Business minor—a combination that positions me uniquely at the intersection of technical innovation and strategic product thinking.

My passion for AI Product Management stems from a fundamental belief: the best products emerge when engineers understand business impact, and product leaders understand technical constraints. I'm not just building skills in isolation; I'm cultivating a mindset that bridges disciplines.

Currently, I work as an Undergraduate Research Assistant in the MIME (Mechanical, Industrial, and Manufacturing Engineering) lab, where I explore hardware-software integration and automation challenges. This role has given me invaluable perspective on how AI and software solutions must harmonize with physical systems and real-world constraints.

Whether I'm optimizing algorithms in Python, analyzing market trends, or collaborating with multidisciplinary research teams, I approach every problem with both technical rigor and product intuition.
